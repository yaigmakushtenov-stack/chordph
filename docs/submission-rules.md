# Submitting a chord chart or tab to chord.ph

These rules keep the shared library clean, singable, and legal to host. Every
submission is reviewed by an admin before it goes into the shared library. Until
it's approved, your version stays private to your own device — you can still use
it, you just won't see it clash with anyone else's copy.

## What you can submit

- **Chord charts** — chords over lyrics, section labels (`[Verse]`, `[Chorus]`).
- **Tabs** — for a specific instrument (lead guitar, bass, piano, etc.). You
  must pick the instrument so people can find the right one.

Mark clearly which one it is. A lead-guitar tab and a rhythm chord chart for the
same song are two different submissions, not one.

## Required for every submission

1. **Correct song title and artist.** Use the original recording artist, not the
   cover you learned it from.
2. **A key.** The key the chart is actually written in.
3. **A reference link** — a YouTube or Spotify link to the recording your chart
   is based on. This is how the reviewer cross-checks that the chords are right.
   No reference = not reviewed.
4. **Instrument** (for tabs) — lead guitar, rhythm guitar, bass, keys/piano, etc.
5. **Section labels.** At least mark the main sections so it's usable live.

## Formatting

- Put chords on their own line, directly above the lyric they change on.
- Use standard chord names: `G`, `Em`, `C#m`, `Dsus4`, `A/C#`, `Bm7`.
- One song per submission.
- Keep it playable — a real, ordered chart someone could lead worship from, not
  a scratch pad.

## What gets rejected

- **Duplicates.** If a good version already exists, submit an *improvement* to it
  rather than a second copy. The reviewer checks for duplicates first.
- **No reference link**, or a reference that doesn't match the chart.
- **Wrong or made-up chords**, placeholder text, joke entries, or spam.
- **Full copyrighted lyrics with no chords** — chord.ph hosts *charts* (chords +
  the lyric cues needed to follow them), not lyric reprints. Keep lyrics to what a
  player needs to play along.
- Anything hateful, offensive, or unrelated to the song.

## How review works

1. You submit. Your version is usable on your device immediately (private).
2. An admin sees it in the review queue with **your email**, the reference link,
   and the chart, and checks it against the recording and for duplicates.
3. If it's good, it's approved into the shared library for everyone.
4. Once it's public, people can **thumbs-up** the versions they trust, so the
   best chart for each song rises to the top.

## A note on ownership

You're submitting your transcription of a song you don't own the copyright to.
By submitting, you confirm the chart is your own work (or you have the right to
share it) and you're okay with it being shown to other chord.ph users for free.
Songwriters/publishers retain all rights to the underlying songs.
